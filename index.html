'How big this cake is!' 'What a' (Exclamation)

Choosing from the constructions you suggest, my most likely response would be "What a big cake!" My impression is that the "How + adjective" construction is no longer active in American English. It would certainly be understood, but a native speaker would produce it only in fixed phrases or with a sarcastic tone.
